Name: BritBuzz

2. Data Processing Purposes

  • Providing news aggregation services
  • User authentication and account management
  • Improving service quality
  • Legal compliance
  • Preventing misuse and ensuring platform security

3. Legal Basis for Processing

We process your personal data based on consent (Article 6(1)(a) GDPR). You have the right to withdraw your consent at any time. Where applicable, processing may also occur under legal obligation (Article 6(1)(c)) or legitimate interest (Article 6(1)(f)) when necessary for security and fraud prevention.

4. Types of Data We Collect

  • Identity Data: Email address (for account creation)
  • Authentication Data: Password (hashed and encrypted)
  • Technical Data: IP address (for security and analytics)
  • Consent Records: Explicit consent for data processing
  • Usage Data: Anonymized usage statistics
  • Children's Data: Our service is not intended for users under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children.

5. Your Rights Under GDPR

Right to Access (Article 15)

You have the right to obtain confirmation of whether we process your personal data and access to that data.

Right to Rectification (Article 16)

You have the right to have inaccurate personal data corrected and incomplete data completed.

Right to Erasure (Article 17)

You have the right to have your personal data deleted ("right to be forgotten").

Right to Portability (Article 20)

You have the right to receive your data in a structured, commonly used format.

Right to Object (Article 21)

You have the right to object to the processing of your personal data.

Right to Restriction (Article 18)

You have the right to restrict the processing of your personal data.

6. Data Retention

  • Account Data: Until account deletion or 1 year of inactivity
  • Session Data: 24 hours or until logout
  • Analytics Data: 26 months (anonymized)
  • Consent Records: 7 years (legal requirement)

7. Data Sharing

Third Parties: We do not share personal data with third parties.

News Sources: We only aggregate public news content.

Analytics: We only use anonymized usage statistics.

Processors: We may use trusted service providers (e.g., cloud hosting, analytics, email services) under GDPR-compliant agreements.

International Transfers: If data is transferred outside the UK/EU, appropriate safeguards such as Standard Contractual Clauses (SCCs) are applied.

8. Security Measures

  • Encryption: All data encrypted in transit and at rest
  • Access Control: Limited access to authorized personnel only
  • Monitoring: Regular security audits and monitoring
  • Data Minimization: Only necessary data is collected

9. Cookies

Essential Cookies

Required for basic website functionality (session cookies, authentication cookies).

Analytics Cookies

Help us understand how users interact with our service (anonymized usage tracking).

Preference Cookies

Remember user settings and preferences (language settings, display preferences).

10. Data Breach Procedure

  • Detection: Automated monitoring and manual review
  • Assessment: Risk assessment within 24 hours
  • Notification: ICO within 72 hours if high risk
  • Mitigation: Immediate containment and security measures
